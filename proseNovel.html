Announcing 'School of X,' the Latest 'Xavier's Institute' Prose Novel The anthology is on sale December 7, 2021!

School's back in session.

The next prose novel in Marvel and Aconyte Books' XAVIER'S INSTITUTE line is School of X, an original X-Men fiction anthology.

In School of X, edited by Gwendolyn Nix, the next generation of the astonishing X-Men face their deadliest foes yet — alternate versions of themselves, in this wide-ranging Marvel anthology featuring seven new original stories. This novel will be available everywhere books are sold on December 7, 2021. Pre-order the title now!
